A yummy spicy chicken tika curry

Chicken tikka masala is a dish consisting of roasted marinated chicken chunks (chicken tikka) in a spiced sauce. The sauce is usually creamy and orange-coloured. The dish was popularised by cooks from South Asia living in Great Britain and is offered at restaurants around the world.

Ingredients

  • 4 onions
  • Masala Paste
  • Red peppers
  • Chicken breasts
  • Tomato Puree
